Why choose us?

Doubleday Expressions Limited was incorporated in February 1992, as a wholly Nigerian Company RC188535. We started operations in 1993. We are a member of NASME and registered ISO Management Systems by Standards Organization of Nigeria SON Doubleday Expressions Limited acquired Franchise Partnership of GCERTI, South Korea to certify companies / organizations in ISO Management Systems Standards: QMS ISO 9001, EMS ISO 14001 and OHSAS 18001. As a multi-award winning organisation we specialise in working with SMEs and big businesses throughout Nigeria, Africa and worldwide to implement and certificate to the core ISO Management Standards. We offer a simple, cost effective service to help businesses achieve ISO Certification.

Our passion is to inspire organisational improvement and efficiency by providing unparalleled support. We allow our customers to put ISO at the heart of what they do.
