CLASSROOM TRAINING

Are you new to an ISO standard? Are you looking to introduce a new member to your ISO team? Maybe you are thinking about certification but want to learn more about the standard before taking the leap?

Our classroom style training is an ideal place to start if you need to know the requirements of a particular standard. We will take you through each clause of your chosen standard, explain in simple terms what each clause means, and then use practical exercises to start applying the theory to real-life scenarios.

What’s more, you can either book a place on one a courses here at our office in Lagos, or, if you’re not able to travel, you can opt for one of our online courses instead.
